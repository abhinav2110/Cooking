APPAM
⌚ 30 mins
♨ EASY
★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ 4
🍽 Servings: 4

Play with your food! Adjust amounts based on your tastes.


INGREDIENTS

  1. Raw rice - 1 & ½ cup
     
  2. Idli rice - 1 & ½ cup
     
  3. Urad dal/vellai ulundhu whole - ⅛ cup
     
  4. Fenugreek seeds/vendhayam - 1 tbsp
     
  5. Salt - As needed
     
  6. Cooked rice or Poha/aval - ¼ cup
    7.  
  7. Cooking soda - 1 generous pinch
     

METHOD

STEP 1:
You can soak and grind everything together too. But make sure it is ground really smooth.

STEP 2:
(OR) Soak both rice, dal in one vessel along with poha and urad dal, fenugreek seeds in another vessel for 3 hrs. First grind fenugreek till smooth and fluffy adding around ½ cup water,then add rest of the soaked items, enough water and grind till smooth.

STEP 3:
Mix salt and keep for fermentation overnight.Batter should be little bit watery than idly batter (just pouring consistency) and should not be heavy,should be light.

STEP 4:
So take required amount of batter, add water or coconut milk for authentic taste to dilute it..

STEP 5:
Before you decide to make aapam,add the cooking soda and stir well.

STEP 6:
Heat a heavy bottomed kadai and pour 1 ½ laddle of batter.Just spread in the sides of the pan like we spread dosa,leaving little batter still in the middle( I find this method easier and quicker).This will give you thin edges,just like if we rotate the pan.

STEP 7:
If  you are convenient in rotating the pan,then hold the two sides of the pan and rotate in a circular motion to get a thin edge.

STEP 8:
If you use oil just drizzle a few drops after u make the aapam,if you do so before then batter will not spread evenly.

STEP 9:
Cook covered in a medium low flame and serve hot with your choice of side dish,stew or kurma or simply coconut milk!
