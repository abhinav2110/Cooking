CAPRESE SALAD

CAPRESE SALAD





⌚ 15 mins
♨ Easy
★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ rating
🍽 Servings: 2

INGREDIENTS

  1. 3 Tomatoes
  2. 8 ounces Mozzarella cheese
  3. Fresh basil
  4. 2 tbsp Olive oil
  5. 1 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
  6. salt
  7. Pepper
    8.

DIRECTIONS

STEP1
Cut slices of tomato and mozzarella.

STEP2
Add a slice of tomato to a plate, then add a slice of mozzarella on top. Keep alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella until you've used up all the ingredients.Place fresh basil leaves in between the layers. I prefer smaller basil leaves to keep them whole, but if you have large basil leaves you can cut them up as well.

STEP3
Drizzle the olive oil and balsamic reduction on top of the tomatoes and mozzarella.

STEP4
Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then serve immediately.
