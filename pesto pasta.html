PESTO PASTA

PESTO PASTA





⌚ 30 mins
♨Medium
★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ rating
🍽 Servings: 2



INGREDIENTS

  1. Almonds
  2. Basil
  3. Extra virgin olive oil
  4. ½ cup chopped onion
  5. 2 ½ tablespoons pesto
  6. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  7. 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  8. 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  9. salt to taste
  10. ground black pepper to taste
    11.

DIRECTIONS

STEP1
In a food processor, blend together basil leaves, nuts, garlic, and cheese.

STEP2
Pour in oil slowly while still mixing. Stir in salt and pepper.

STEP3
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done.

STEP4
Drain.Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan over medium low heat. Add pesto, onion, and salt and pepper. Cook about five minutes, or until onions are soft.

STEP5
In a large bowl, mix pesto mixture into pasta. Stir in grated cheese. Serve.
